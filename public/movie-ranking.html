The Best Movies According to Connor

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spiderman Far From Home

Has some awesome action scenes, a great hero teammup, and an the most ultimate villian teamup of all time. The best superhero movie of all time.

The Sixth Sense

A cleverly crafted suspensful horror that keeps you at the edge of your seat throughout the full length of the movie

The Marvels

An awesome new superhero movie where 3 unlikely heros teamup to fight the force of evil in what will hopefully become a classic one day.
